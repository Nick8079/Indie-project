The Evil Dwarves

For many millenia the Duergar (Evil Race of Dwarves) have enslaved dragons to light their forges. By torturing the dragons, forcing them to breath fire for many days at a time, the Duergar have crafted some of the strongest weaponsknown in the land. Over time the Duergar have acquired wealth and power like no other race. Longing for freedom, the last dragon known to exist in the realm remain enslaved.
